Hello Everyone!
My name is Hawk Shannon. I am originally from Colorado Springs, CO. I have made my way across the United States in various moves. After high school, I attended Chadron State College, located in Chadron, NE. After a year of attendence there I moved to Bozeman, MT and attended Montana State College. From there I landeded in Chicago, IL, where I began carrer in the Restaurant/Hospitality industry.

I eventually made my way to Philadelphia, where I decided that a career change was necessary to sustain longevity in the workforce. I watched a very imformative VICE news program that touched on the importance of Coding in the future and felt that would be something that I could be sucessful.
